Crusty Pizza

If Italy is known for one thing, it is pizza.

Pizza is a dish that can be eaten for lunch, dinner and for students, even for breakfast.

But the creation of a delicious pizza is some science in itself.

With this recipe, you can extingiush your lusting thoughts for pizza very quickly.

Ingredients

  • A scissor
  • A Freezer or a supermarket

How to achieve a warm pizza

  1. Check your freezer. Any good households have at least 5 frozen pizzas for emergencies in their freezer.
    • If you are brave, you can also check behind your fridge for a yummy good good.
    • In the unlikely event that there is no pizza, locate the nearest super market and buy a frozen pizza there.
  2. Get your scissor to open the plastic wrapping that divides you from the important food item
    • You may be tempted to eat your pizza now but beware, it is a lot more tastier and easy to chew if it is not frozen

  3. Put the pizza into the oven. You can either preheat or heat the pizza together with your oven.

    Some people have terrible ovens, others are misguided by the paradox of the preheated oven, some just choose to follow any order they find on the back of a package.

    Just be brave, explore new ideas. At the very least, get the pizza into a hotter state than it was in the freezer

  4. Once the cheese is melted and the crust turns browner than it was after it was deeply frozen, get the pizza out of the oven.

    Some people have special tools for that. Any student knows however, that you can get a pizza out with almost anything. A go to device for them is the spork.

And there you have it. Italy's biggest "accomplishment" done in like 15 to 30 minutes or so.
