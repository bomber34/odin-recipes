Delicious Ice Cream

Ice cream is one of the most popular desserts in the world.

Therefore, any famous chef cook should provide a recipe how people can get their hands on a delicious treat.

The steps are quite simple and easy to follow

Ingredients

  • Some spare coins

Recipe

  1. Locate your nearest italian caf√©
  2. Go up to the ice cream counter
  3. For the first few words of the order, try to speak confidently and order some gelato, before you realize that you have some social anxiety
  4. Forget the names of your favorite flavors. Instead use your finger and order some of "this" and "uhhh that"
    • Don't forget to keep your budget in mind. You should only order at max the number of scoops of your favorite "uhhh that" that you can pay for
  5. Pay with your coins. The more coins you have, the more ice cream you get :)

Keep in mind that ice cream lasts longer in colder regions.

Real pros will ask for a napkin, to avoid melted ice cream on their hands!
