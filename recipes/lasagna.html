Simple Lasagna

This easy recipe allows anyone with too much time and hunger to create a lasagna that is at least edible.

The ingredients are cheap and the steps are quick to read!

Also no bechamel sauce. Because I don't want to put too much effort into this.

ingredients

  • Pasta plates
  • Ground meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • Some cheese
  • A single leaf of basil

How to cook some lasagna

  1. Spice the ground meat with enough salt and pepper. When in doubt, put more salt, pepper and other spices into this bland ingredient
  2. Turn on your stove and heat a pan
  3. Put the ground meat into the pan until it is nice and brown
  4. Once the meat is ready, pour the tomato sauce on top of it and mix
  5. Get a lasagna bowl and layer up like this
    1. Pasta plate
    2. meat
    3. repeat with step 5.1 until you run out of meat
    4. Top the lasagna with a final pasta plate
    5. Add cheese on top of your lasagna
  6. Put the lasagna into the oven for 30min at 180°C
  7. Once the lasagna is out of the oven, decorate it with a stratigically placed leaf of basil
  8. Order some real lasagna next time

And voilá! Making lasagna is just that easy!
